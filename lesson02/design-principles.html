Alignment

  • America First Credit Union
  • www.americafirst.com/

The website is an example of an Alignment Desing as the elements are not randomly places on the website. You can visually see a grid to help with the aligment of the elements on the screen.

Visual Hierarchy

  • MTN OPS
  • mtnops.com

The website is an example of Visual Hierarchy as it is organizies it page in a way that helps guide potential buys of their prodcut to the more important things first. They have things placed in a way that puts the most important links at the top of the page. They have a consistent deign through their website as well.

White Space and Clean Design

  • Select Health
  • selecthealth.org/

The website is an example of the use of white spaces and clean design. You can see on the screen the use of a lot of white space with very simple colors. It is a clean design with not a lot of information on the screen. It has what is needed and not a lot of example design elements.
