Membership Information
Membership Level

Gold Membership

Costs: $1,000 per year

  • Featured listing in the chamber's online directory with priority placement.
  • Four complimentary tickets to chamber events per year.
  • Priority sponsorship opportunities for chamber events.
  • Access to VIP networking events with key stakeholders and community leaders.
  • Quarterly feature in chamber newsletter.

Silver Membership

Costs: $500 per year

  • Enhanced listing in the chamber's online directory with logo and website link.
  • Priority placement at chamber events.
  • Two complimentary tickets to one chamber event per year.
  • Access to exclusive member-only workshops and seminars.

Bronze Membership

Costs: $250 per year

  • Basic listing in the chamber's online directory.
  • Access to chamber events at standard member rates.
  • Monthly newsletter subscription.

Non Profit Organization

Costs: Free

  • Basic listing in the chamber's online directory as a nonprofit member.
  • Access to chamber events at discounted nonprofit rates.
  • Access to educational resources and workshops tailored for nonprofits.
